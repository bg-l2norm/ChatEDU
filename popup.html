Getting Started

Welcome! If you're launching for the first time, here are the steps you need to follow to set up the custom instructions in your ChatGPT interface:

  1. Locate and click on the 'Starting Guide' dropdown menu in the side panel.
  2. Navigate through the provided links and copy the custom instruction templates for both User and Model Response.
  3. Paste the copied instructions into the custom instruction panel, which is located at the bottom left of your ChatGPT interface.
